Golden Curry w/ Crispy Shallot Basil Oil

Shallot Basil Oil

  1. Heat 1/2 cup avocado, peanut, and sesame oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Slice 4–5 shallots thinly and fry in 2 batches until golden brown, removing and leaving oil in the pan.
  3. Turn off heat and add 1 Tbsp of oil to a medium Dutch oven.
  4. Add 1/2 cup chopped basil, 1 Tbsp red Thai curry paste, 1 tsp Balti curry seasoning, and a pinch of salt to the leftover oil. This will be your garnish with crispy shallots.

Curry

  1. Marinate 1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs with grated ginger, garlic, curry powder, cumin, coriander, honey, salt, and pepper for 10 minutes to several hours.
  2. In the Dutch oven, heat oil to medium-high and sear chicken in batches, 3 mins per side.
  3. Remove chicken, add shallots, ginger, and garlic; sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add 2 cans coconut milk, soy sauce, Better Than Bouillon, sweet potatoes, carrots, and chicken back in. Add water to cover if needed.
  5. Simmer covered for 15 mins, then uncovered for 10–15 mins.
  6. Add turbinado sugar and lemon juice during final cooking time.

Serve with crispy shallot basil oil and steamed jasmine rice.
