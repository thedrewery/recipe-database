Golden Curry w/ Crispy Shallot Basil Oil

Shallot Basil Oil:

  1. Heat the oil: In a medium saucepan, heat 1/2 cup avocado, peanut, and sesame oil over medium heat.
  2. Fry shallots: Slice 4–5 shallots thinly and fry in 2 batches until golden brown, removing and leaving as much oil behind as possible.
  3. Prepare garnish oil: Turn off heat and add 1 Tbsp of the oil to a medium Dutch oven. Add 1/2 cup chopped basil, 1 Tbsp red Thai curry paste, 1 tsp Balti curry seasoning, and a pinch of salt. Reserve for garnish with the crispy shallots.

Curry:

  1. Marinate the chicken: Combine 1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs with grated ginger, garlic, curry powder, cumin, coriander, honey, salt, and pepper. Marinate for 10 minutes to a few hours refrigerated.
  2. Sear chicken: In the Dutch oven, heat oil to medium-high and sear chicken thighs in 2 batches, about 3 minutes per side, avoiding scorching the bottom. Add more avocado oil if necessary.
  3. Sauté aromatics: Remove chicken and add 2–3 minced shallots, 1 tsp grated ginger, and 4 grated garlic cloves. Cook until softened and fragrant.
  4. Add liquids and vegetables: Stir in 2 cans full-fat coconut milk, 2 Tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp Better Than Bouillon, 2 cups chunked peeled sweet potatoes, and 1.5 cups roughly chopped carrots. Add water to cover if needed.
  5. Simmer: Cover and simmer on low for 15 minutes, then uncover and simmer on medium-low for 10–15 more minutes.
  6. Finish the curry: Stir in 2 Tbsp turbinado sugar and the juice of 1 lemon during the final cooking time.

Serve with the crispy shallot basil oil and steamed jasmine rice.
