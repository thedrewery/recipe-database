33rd St Pesto

Ingredients:

  • ~4 oz basil (1 large bunch)
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro
  • 3 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1 cup grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • ~1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prep the herbs: Remove leaves from stems of both basil and cilantro. Wash and dry thoroughly in a salad spinner.
  2. Load the processor: Add basil and cilantro leaves to a food processor and weigh them down with garlic, pine nuts, and cheese.
  3. Add liquids: Pour in olive oil and lemon juice, then season with salt, pepper, and optional sugar.
  4. Blend: Process until smooth and creamy, scraping down sides as needed.
  5. Store: Transfer to a jar and top with a thin layer of olive oil for best storage.
