Tandoori Butter Carrots

Ingredients:

  • 3–4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 teaspoons good-quality curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried fenugreek leaves
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 shallots, diced
  • 1–2 pounds carrots, peeled and cut into ~3-inch lengths
  • Yogurt sauce (optional, for serving)
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F.
  2. Make spiced butter: Melt 3–4 tablespoons of butter in a small saucepan over low heat.
  3. Add aromatics: Stir in 2 teaspoons curry powder, 1/2 teaspoon each of coriander, turmeric, cumin, and fenugreek, along with the minced garlic and diced shallots.
  4. Sauté: Gently sauté the mixture over low heat until the shallots begin to soften and the garlic just starts to brown.
  5. Prep carrots: Peel the carrots and cut them into roughly 3-inch-long pieces. Spread them in a shallow baking tray.
  6. Season carrots: Pour most of the butter and seasoning mixture over the carrots, reserving most of the shallots. Toss to coat.
  7. Roast: Roast in the oven for 20 minutes. Then add the reserved shallots and roast for another 10 minutes.
  8. Serve: Serve hot with yogurt sauce or enjoy on their own!
