Mediterranean Lamb Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 3 Tbsp chopped Italian parsley
  • 2 Tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 2 Tbsp chopped dill
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 small garlic cloves, grated
  • 2 Tbsp grated onion or shallot
  • 1/2 cup plain gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 3/4 tsp fine Celtic sea salt
  • ~1/2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • pinch of curry powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat: Set oven to 375°F and lightly oil a baking tray.
  2. Prep: Mince parsley, cilantro, dill, and garlic. Grate onion or shallot.
  3. Mix: In a bowl, combine ground lamb, beaten egg, garlic, onion, herbs, breadcrumbs, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Coat your hands with olive oil and gently mix until just combined.
  4. Shape: Roll mixture into golfball-sized meatballs and place evenly on tray.
  5. Bake: Cook for 16–18 minutes, until lightly browned and cooked through.
