Drew’s Lamb Barbacoa

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb lamb stew meat
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo (optional)
  • 2 dried guajillo chiles, seeded
  • 1 dried California chile, seeded
  • Juice of 2 oranges
  • Juice of 2 limes (¾ cup total citrus juice)
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2½ tablespoons honey or light brown sugar
  • 1 cup + 1 tablespoon chicken bone broth
  • 1½ teaspoons cumin
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 1½ teaspoons dried Mexican oregano
  • 2 dried cloves
  • Pinch of allspice
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Avocado oil

Instructions:

  1. Brown the lamb: Set Instant Pot to sauté. Add avocado oil and brown the lamb pieces in batches with salt and pepper—only browning one side. Do not cook through.
  2. Sauté aromatics: Add onion, garlic, and dried chiles to the pot and sauté until slightly charred. Deglaze with 1 cup of bone broth.
  3. Add spices: Stir in cumin, cloves, oregano, paprika, and allspice (reserve bay leaves). Simmer for about 6 minutes.
  4. Blend sauce: Transfer sautéed mixture to a blender. Add orange juice, lime juice, apple cider vinegar, chipotle (if using), and 1 tsp salt. Blend until smooth.
  5. Combine and pressure cook: Arrange browned lamb back in the Instant Pot. Pour blended sauce over lamb and add bay leaves. Pressure cook on high for 20 minutes.
  6. Natural release: Let pressure naturally release for 15 minutes, then quick release the rest.
  7. Reduce sauce: Remove lamb and set aside. Add 1 tablespoon brown sugar or honey to the sauce. Simmer with bay leaves on sauté for 15 minutes to reduce slightly.
  8. Shred and finish: Shred lamb with two forks, discarding excess fat. Return meat to the pot and cook on low for 1–3 more hours. Serve with a slotted spoon.
