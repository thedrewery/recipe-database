Simple Coconut Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup organic jasmine rice
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp Better Than Bouillon
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice: Wash jasmine rice until the water runs mostly clear.
  2. Combine ingredients: Add rice, coconut milk, water, Better Than Bouillon, and sea salt to a pot.
  3. Cook: Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low. Cook for 15 minutes.
  4. Rest: Remove from heat and leave covered for 10 minutes.
  5. Fluff and serve: Fluff rice with a fork before serving.
