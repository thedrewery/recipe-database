Shawarma Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2.5 lbs whole peeled chicken thighs
  • 1 cup whole Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground clove
  • 1 tablespoon Whole Foods Shawarma Seasoning
  • 4 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 large yellow onions, thinly sliced into crescent moons
  • Avocado oil, for greasing

Instructions:

  1. Marinate: In a large bowl, whisk together Greek yogurt, paprika, turmeric, cumin, coriander, ground clove, shawarma seasoning, pressed garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Add chicken thighs and marinate for several hours or overnight.
  2. Prepare pan: Line a baking sheet with tin foil and lightly grease with avocado oil. Spread the sliced onions evenly on the tray, then arrange marinated chicken thighs on top so their juices drip into the onions while baking.
  3. Bake: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Bake for 10 minutes, then flip chicken and continue baking for another 10 minutes, until cooked through.
  4. Slice: Remove from oven and allow chicken to cool slightly before slicing into strips.
  5. Crisp: Return sliced chicken and onions to the tray, toss together, and broil or bake on high for a few minutes to crisp up like authentic shawarma.
