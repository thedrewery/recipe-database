Drew’s Vegan Queso

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw unsalted cashews, soaked overnight in water
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 3/4 tsp to 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 small chipotle pepper in adobo
  • 1 tsp hot sauce (vinegar-based) or apple cider vinegar
  • 1.5 cloves garlic, diced
  • ~1/3 cup filtered water (or more to reach desired consistency)

Instructions

  1. Place 1 cup of raw unsalted cashews in a small container and cover with water. Soak overnight.
  2. Drain the soaked cashews and transfer to a blender (a small Ninja attachment works well).
  3. Add the lime juice, sea salt, cumin, chili powder, chipotle pepper, hot sauce or vinegar, garlic, and about 1/3 cup of filtered water.
  4. Blend until smooth, adding more water a little at a time to achieve your desired queso consistency.
