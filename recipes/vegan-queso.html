Vegan Queso

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup raw cashews (soaked if not using high-speed blender)
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened plant milk (like oat or almond)
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 Tbsp mellow white miso
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tsp turmeric (optional, for color)
  • 1 tsp white vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 tsp tapioca starch (optional, for stretchiness)
  • Splash of pickled jalapeño brine (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a high-speed blender and blend until completely smooth.
  2. Transfer to a saucepan and cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly.
  3. Cook until warmed through and slightly thickened — about 3–5 minutes. If using tapioca, it will become stretchy.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt, acid, or jalapeño brine if needed.
  5. Serve warm with chips, on nachos, or as a dip for veggies.
