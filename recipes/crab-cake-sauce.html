Drew’s Ocean Ave Seafood Crab Cake Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1–2 red bell peppers, minced and patted dry
  • 1–2 shallots, minced
  • 1/4 cup sweet onion
  • Butter (for sautéing)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock or bone broth
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • 1 tablespoon Grey Poupon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons whole grain mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup half and half

Instructions:

  1. Sauté aromatics: In a pan, sauté the minced red bell pepper, shallots, and sweet onion in butter over medium heat. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until slightly browned.
  2. Deglaze and simmer: Add 1/2 cup chicken stock (or bone broth) and 1/3 cup white wine. Stir and bring to a gentle simmer.
  3. Blend and reserve texture: Blend the mixture, reserving a small handful of the sautéed vegetables for added texture.
  4. Reduce: Return the blended sauce to the pan and simmer gently for about 15 minutes, allowing it to reduce and concentrate.
  5. Add flavor: Stir in 1 tablespoon Grey Poupon Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons whole grain mustard, and 1 tablespoon lemon juice. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  6. Finish: Stir in 1/3 cup half and half and gently warm through before serving.
