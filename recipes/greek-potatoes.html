Greek Lemon Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp sea salt
  • 2 lbs Yukon gold potatoes, chopped into 1–2 inch chunks
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 Tbsp dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat: Set oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Mix: In a bowl, combine olive oil, chicken broth, lemon juice, and sea salt. Pour mixture over potatoes and toss to coat.
  3. Roast: Spread potatoes on a baking tray and roast for 35 minutes, flipping halfway through, until liquid is mostly evaporated.
  4. Toss: Remove from oven and toss with grated garlic and dried oregano.
  5. Finish: Return to oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until golden and fragrant.
