Gloria’s Lamb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 10 black peppercorns
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 6 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Drizzle of honey

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients: In a bowl or resealable bag, mix together red wine, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, bay leaf, curry powder, cumin, lemon juice, honey, and crushed peppercorns.
  2. Marinate: Add the lamb (half a leg works best) and ensure it’s well coated. Marinate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Grill: Grill over medium-high heat, turning occasionally until nicely browned and cooked to your preferred doneness.
  4. Rest and serve: Let the lamb rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
