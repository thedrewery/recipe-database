Killer Peanut Sauce For Noods

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and grated (or a couple frozen ginger cubes)
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated (or a couple frozen garlic cubes)
  • 1 tsp peanut oil
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp sambal oelek
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 Tbsp gluten-free tamari
  • Juice of 1 lime (about 2 Tbsp)
  • 1/3 cup hot water, plus more as needed for consistency
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Sauté aromatics: Heat peanut oil and toasted sesame oil in a small pan over low heat. Add garlic and ginger and sauté until soft and translucent, not browned.
  2. Combine: Transfer the garlic and ginger to a bowl. Add peanut butter, sambal oelek, honey, tamari, lime juice, and salt.
  3. Thin: Stir in hot water until smooth and saucy, adding more as needed to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Use: Toss with noodles, drizzle over vegetables, or use as a dipping sauce.
