Easy Chilaquiles a la Susan Fenninger

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup half and half (or cream or milk)
  • ½ cup good salsa (molcajete, salsa verde, or another favorite)
  • 4 oz (or more) tortilla chips
  • ⅓ cup grated cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Fresh chopped peppers and tomatoes
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Cotija cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, scramble, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a non-stick pan over medium-high heat, add the half and half. Let it simmer and reduce slightly.
  3. Stir in the salsa and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Add tortilla chips and stir to coat evenly. The longer they cook, the softer they become—work quickly for a mix of textures.
  5. Once chips are coated, stir in grated cheese until melted.
  6. Add chopped peppers and tomatoes, stir to combine.
  7. Serve immediately and garnish with fresh cilantro, cotija cheese, and extra salsa or fermented hot sauce if desired.
