Easy Chilaquiles a la Susan Fenninger

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup half and half, cream, or milk
  • 1/2 cup good salsa (molcajete, verde, etc.)
  • 4 oz or more tortilla chips
  • 1/3 cup grated cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Fresh chopped peppers and tomatoes
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Cotija (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, scramble, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Turn a non-stick pan to medium-high heat and add cream, milk, or half and half.
  3. Once simmering and beginning to reduce, stir in the salsa.
  4. Add tortilla chips and mix into the sauce. Work quickly to avoid over-softening.
  5. Add grated cheese and stir to combine.
  6. Stir in chopped peppers and tomatoes.
  7. Plate and garnish with cilantro, cotija, and extra salsa or fermented hot sauce.
