Drew’s Lightning Strikes The Pope Italian Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup plain gluten-free bread crumbs
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 lb organic grass fed ground beef
  • 2 hot Italian pork sausages (casings removed)
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • Ample fresh ground black pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1/3 cup very fine minced red onion (or grated with some excess moisture removed)
  • 3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
  • 1.5 tablespoons minced fresh basil
  • Drizzle of olive oil
  • Sprinkle of lemon zest and Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat oven to 365°F and prepare a parchment-lined tray.
  2. Combine ingredients: Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, coat your hands with EVOO, and mix thoroughly with your hands.
  3. Form meatballs: Roll mixture into golf ball sized balls (about 24 total) and place evenly on the prepared tray.
  4. Bake and serve: Bake for 28 minutes or until meatballs are fully cooked. Serve with Nonna Sauce and extra Parmesan if you’d like.
