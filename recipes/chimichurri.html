Drew’s Chimichurri

Ingredients

  • 1 cup minced cilantro (including some stems)
  • 1/2 cup minced parsley (no stems)
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Pinch of cumin
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1.5 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 3 cloves grated garlic
  • 1/4 cup finely minced shallot or red onion
  • 1 Tbsp turbinado or other raw sugar
  • Olive oil (to desired consistency)

Instructions

  1. Start with garlic and lime juice in a bowl.
  2. Mince the herbs (cilantro and parsley) and add to the bowl.
  3. Add the red pepper flakes, cumin, oregano, and salt.
  4. Pour in red wine vinegar and mix well.
  5. Add shallot/onion and olive oil to reach desired consistency.
  6. Stir in sugar and adjust to taste.
