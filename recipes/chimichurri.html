Drew’s Chimichurri

Ingredients

  • 1 cup minced cilantro (including some stems)
  • 1/2 cup minced parsley (no stems)
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Pinch of cumin
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1.5 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1/4 cup finely minced shallot or red onion
  • 1 Tbsp turbinado sugar (or other raw sugar)
  • Olive oil (to desired consistency)

Instructions

  1. Start by mixing the grated garlic and lime juice together in a bowl.
  2. Mince the cilantro and parsley and add them to the bowl.
  3. Add red pepper flakes, cumin, oregano, and salt. Stir to combine.
  4. Add the red wine vinegar, shallot or red onion, and enough olive oil to create a loose sauce.
  5. Stir in the turbinado sugar and adjust to taste.
