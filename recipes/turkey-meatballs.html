A Man Who Knows Meatballs: Turkey Edition

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb higher-fat ground turkey
  • 1 small yellow onion, minced or grated with excess moisture removed
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped parsley
  • 1 Tbsp finely chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 1/3 cup chopped golden raisins
  • 1/2 cup fine gluten-free bread crumbs
  • A few pinches ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • A few pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • Olive oil (for coating hands)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat: Heat oven to 365°F and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Mix: In a large bowl, combine all ingredients until well incorporated. Coat your hands lightly with olive oil and mix gently to avoid compacting the meat.
  3. Shape: Form the mixture into golf ball–sized meatballs and arrange evenly on the tray.
  4. Bake: Bake for 12–14 minutes, until cooked through and lightly browned.
